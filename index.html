Who are we

JAMII COMPASSIONATE hold several values close to our hearts, for prospects and partners. We take responsibility; have passion for ensuring reliable Funeral Services to our members. We are continually diligent to ensure the best solution with the expertise of our valued staffs, which are knowledgeable, compassionate and loyal. We believe in family value, maintaining health and promoting positivity as well as helping the financially challenged individuals.

Objectives

To empower our members financially
To promote a sense of belonging for our members to live as a family
To assist members and their beneficiaries with funeral expenses

 

Our Vision

To help Kenyans living in urban and semi-rural set-up to get empowered by providing a descent send-off, in the event of unforeseen eventualities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the number one, last respect service provider in Kenya and East Africa at large.
